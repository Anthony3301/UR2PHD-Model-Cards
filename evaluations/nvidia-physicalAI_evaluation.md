## Overall Comments  
The PhysicalAI-Autonomous-Vehicles dataset by NVIDIA is **impressively large, technically detailed, and well suited for cutting-edge autonomous vehicle research**. It stands out for its multi-sensor coverage, long clip durations, and global geographic spread. On the flip side, several transparency elements remain under-documented, notably the annotation verification process, demographic coverage of scenarios, and formal versioning history. For high-stakes deployment or fairness audits, these details would enhance trust and reusability. Overall, this dataset card is a strong example, and with a few enhancements it could meet many of the best-practice standards in dataset documentation.
